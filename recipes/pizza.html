Pizza

Description

This easy pizza recipe features a homemade dough that's crispy on the outside and chewy on the inside. Topped with a rich tomato sauce, gooey mozzarella cheese, and your favorite toppings, it's perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 1 1/2 cups warm water
  • 3 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup pizza sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Your favorite toppings (pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, etc.)

Directions

  1. In a small bowl, dissolve yeast in warm water and let it sit for about 5 minutes until foamy.
  2. In a large bowl, combine flour, olive oil, sugar, and salt. Add the yeast mixture and knead until a smooth dough forms. Let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour.
  3. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C). Roll out the dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  4. Spread pizza sauce over the dough, sprinkle with mozzarella cheese, and add your favorite toppings.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
