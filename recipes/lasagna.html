Lasagna

Description

I suppose since I have recipes for Skillet Lasagna, Lasagna Soup, Lasagna Roll Ups, and Taco Lasagna, we are long overdue for me to share my easy, classic lasagna recipe. This recipe has simple ingredients but tons of flavor with a creamy ricotta mixture, savory meat sauce, and lots of mozzarella cheese. This is a great freezer meal, make-ahead recipe, and leftovers freeze and reheat super well.

Ingredients

Cheese Filling
  • 15 oz. ricotta cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup parmesean cheese
  • 2 teaspoons Italian seasoning
  • 1 teaspoons salt
  • 1 teaspoons pepper
Meat Sauce
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 yellow onion
  • 1 lb. ground beef
  • 1 lb ground Italian sausage
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 40 oz. marinara sauce
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon hot sauce
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
Lasagna Noodles/Cheese Topping
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 2.5 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

Directions

  1. Spoon meat sauce on the bottom of a lightly greased casserole dish. Add 4 boiled lasagna noodles (or use no-boil or fresh noodles). Spread 1/3 of the ricotta cheese mixture on top. Add 1.5 cups of meat sauce.
  2. Repeat the same layers two more times.
  3. Top with mozzarella cheese. Cover and bake at 375Â° for 30 minutes. Remove cover and bake for 15 more minutes. Broil at the end if desired.
